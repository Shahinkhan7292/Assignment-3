Business Opportunities In Bangladesh





Last Updated on September 2, 2021 by Shahin Khan


Bangladesh has a population of over 160 Million and it is the 8th most populous country in the world. Bangladesh is located in the southern-eastern part of Asia and its economy is very progressive. Moreover, Bangladesh has a strong and large consumer group, low production cost, and last but not least a huge manpower to utilize. All these key factors have led international companies to focus on Bangladesh.

Bangladesh is the birthplace of microfinance banking and the Government of Bangladesh is doing all it can to encourage foreign investors. There are several export processing zones available in Bangladesh. So if you are interested to come to Bangladesh to open a business then it is guaranteed that you are coming to a safer business environment compared to many other countries of the world.
